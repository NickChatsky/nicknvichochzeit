Our Story

In the beautiful tapestry of life, two hearts found each other across cultures and languages. Victorine and Nikita's love story began as a testament to the power of connection that transcends borders.

"Jesus was standing between us, holding my hand and yours. We are truly one in Him"

From their first meeting to this sacred moment, their journey has been guided by faith, love, and the beautiful blend of French elegance and Russian warmth. Together, they have discovered that love speaks all languages and that two hearts can become one under God's blessing.

As they prepare to unite their lives in marriage, they invite you to witness this celebration of love, faith, and the beginning of their new chapter together in Berlin.
